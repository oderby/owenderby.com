Owen Derby


About me: I’m an engineer: I like solving problems and fixing things. Whether it’s tracking down a bug or building a new sub-system, I’m adept at jumping into a variety of different projects and producing results. I prefer building backends and systems, but find myself producing a lot of javascript lately (a necessary evil of building things for the web). Detail-oriented and thorough, I take pride in my work.

When I’m not working, I like traveling, taking photos, and playing a variety of games with friends (so long as it involves more thinking than randomness). I also care about having a positive impact on our planet and society. Currently, I’m helping build and run 5calls.org, but I am always looking for other ways to get involved.

I’m actively seeking new full-time engineering opportunities at this time. If you have a role you’d like to discuss, please get in touch. While I’m most passionate about having an impact on climate change, I’m interested in any opportunity to have an impact outside this little tech bubble which I occupy.
